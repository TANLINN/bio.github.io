I am a Ph.D. candidate at the [College of Computer Science](http://www.cs.cqu.edu.cn/), Chongqing University, China. My Ph.D. supervisor at Chongqing University is Prof. Songtao Guo. Currently, I am a Visiting Ph.D. Student at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore, supervised by Prof. Dusit Niyato and Prof. Jun Zhao. Previously, I obtained my M.S. degree from Central South University of Forestry and Technology.

My research interests lie in the intersection of mobile computing and artificial intelligence. Specifically, I focus on:

  • Collaborative Edge Intelligence
  • On-Device Model Inference
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning Improvements & Applications
  • Aerial and Space assisted Edge Computing (UAV, RIS, Satellite)

Research Experience

My research focuses on optimizing performance and resource utilization in complex computing networks.

  • On-Device AI Model Inference Optimization: I proposed a GCN-driven evolutionary reinforcement learning method to address on-device model inference by adaptively partitioning neural network layers across edge nodes, considering resource constraints.
  • Intelligent Scheduling in Aerial and Space assisted Networks: I have designed several novel algorithms for emerging network architectures, including an improved DE+TD3 algorithm for multi-UAV deployment, a hybrid-action TD3 algorithm for RIS-assisted edge computing, and a multi-agent MATD3 architecture for hierarchical scheduling in Ground-Air-Space networks.
  • Offloading and Resource Optimization in Edge Computing: I formulated a bi-level optimization framework to solve delay-aware offloading and designed a novel D2QN-based edge collaboration strategy to handle decentralized task scheduling under dynamic workloads.
